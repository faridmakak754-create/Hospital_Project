Privacy Policy

Welcome to Farid Institute of Medical Science. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our online services.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information:

  • Name, contact number, email address
  • Date of birth, gender
  • Medical history or health-related details (only when you provide them voluntarily, e.g., during appointment booking)

b. Non-Personal Information:

  • Browser type and version
  • IP address and device information
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and website usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Register and manage your patient account
  • Schedule appointments and manage medical records
  • Send notifications and updates related to your care
  • Improve our website and patient services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Data Protection and Security

We use secure servers, encrypted connections (SSL), and strict data access controls to protect your information. However, please note that no online system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Sharing of Information

We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With authorized doctors and staff for providing medical care
  • With third-party service providers (like payment gateways) under strict confidentiality
  • When required by law or government authorities

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal or medical record requirements)
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

You can make such requests by contacting us through the details below.
