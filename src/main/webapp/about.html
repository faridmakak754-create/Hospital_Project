About Us

The Farid Institute of Medical Science (FIMS) was founded in 2020 under the visionary leadership of Mr. Farid, the Director of the institute. Located in Lilong, Thoubal District, Manipur, our institution is dedicated to delivering exceptional healthcare services and promoting advanced medical education and research.

At FIMS, we believe that healthcare is not just about treatment — it's about care, compassion, and commitment. Our team of skilled doctors, nurses, and medical professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives the highest standard of care in a safe and healing environment.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to every individual while nurturing a new generation of medical professionals who are guided by ethics, knowledge, and empathy.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading centers of medical excellence in Northeast India — combining modern technology with compassionate care to improve lives and strengthen communities.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical & Surgical Services

Well-Equipped Diagnostic & Emergency Units

Patient-Centered Care

Medical Education & Training

Community Health Initiatives

Our Commitment

We are committed to upholding the values of trust, respect, and service. Every patient at the Farid Institute of Medical Science is treated with dignity and compassion — because we believe that every life deserves the best care possible.

Our Promise: Quality healthcare with compassion and integrity.
